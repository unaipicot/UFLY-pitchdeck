AI Autonomy

UFLY AI

Trained in a Custom Physics Engine

1

Custom Simulation

Standard sims lack fidelity. We built a custom aero engine with non-linear CL-α stall modeling so the AI learns real stall recovery.

2

Curriculum Learning

AI agent trained via PPO. Phase 1: Stabilization (200k steps). Phase 2: Navigation. Phase 3: Wind disturbances & Domain Randomization.

3

Full 4D Control

Direct continuous control over Ailerons, Elevator, Rudder, and Throttle based on a 20-dim sensor observation space.
